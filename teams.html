Yunus Bashir

CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER HADBASH MINING COMPANY LIMITED

Yunus Bashir is a distinguished Mining Engineer and Chief Executive Officer of Hadbash Mining Company Limited. He has a proven track record in shaping mining policy, driving sector reforms, fostering partnerships in mineral trading and agriculture, and advising on strategic projects within Nigeria’s extractives industry.
He holds a National Diploma in Mineral and Petroleum Resources Engineering and a Higher National Diploma in Mining Engineering from Kaduna Polytechnic. He is a member of the Nigeria Society of Mining Engineers (NSME), Nigeria Association of Technologists in Engineering (NATE), Society of Petroleum Engineers (SPE), and the Society for Mining, Metallurgy & Exploration (SME).
