About Us

Hadbash Mining Company Limited is a Nigerian-based mining and extractives firm committed to excellence in the exploration, production, and strategic development of mineral resources and agricultural linkages. With deep technical expertise, policy insight, and a partnership-driven approach, the company stands as a catalyst for sustainable mining growth across Nigeria’s mineral sector.

Our Mission

  • To explore and develop mineral assets with integrity, efficiency, and safety.
  • To build strategic partnerships across the mineral and agricultural value chains.
  • To provide expert advisory and policy support in the extractive industries.
  • To promote responsible, sustainable, and inclusive mining practices.

Our Vision

To be a leading indigenous mining enterprise driving sustainable value creation, supporting Nigeria’s mineral development goals, and empowering local communities.

Our Achievement

Achievement Proud to announce a partnership between Hadbash Mining Company Nigeria Ltd (RC 6873407), Spintex Heritage International Ltd (RC 1489329), and Greenstone 4 Royal Seal Ltd (RC 8167894) to supply 10,000 metric tons of kaolinite per month. This collaboration will boost our production capacity and support local industry growth.
